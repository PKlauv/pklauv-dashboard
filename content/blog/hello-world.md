# Dashboard? Portfolio?
## Let's figure it out

I had this idea where I could share my thought processes to somewhere where nobody would look normally, somewhere where I could just blurt on and ramble about something I'm interested in. 

But at the same time, it would be nice to see if even someone takes even just a *couple* minutes out of their day to see what I'm up to. 

My original thought process for this was to have a dashboard where I could see analytics that are personal to me and could *"stalk"* my other repositories' activity, but it turned out into something bigger, a larger plan with more ideas.

I see now an idea of a website that can make visitors more engaged with my content and my activity, as I will be trying to widen my knowledge in the future, and sharing that path from time to time. 

I've gotten some inspiration from this developer I respect for my ideas and approach.
